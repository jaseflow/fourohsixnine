About

With years of experience and a loyal client base, Clinton’s strong ambition and his genuine, down-to-earth approach with people has made him a valued member of the 4069 team and respected in the real estate industry.

Always seeking to learn and broaden his skill set, Clinton has a level of experience and tenacity that has seen him produce outstanding results and earn a solid reputation in the area. Throughout his career, his involvement across multiple facets of real estate from residential sales to large scale developments has given him a breadth of knowledge that allows him to offer a more specialised service tailored to his client’s specific needs.

From a financial background, Clinton uses analytical thinking and logic to provide relevant information his clients can trust and draws on his refined ability to assess the market in order to help find viable solutions for his clients and assist them navigate their property options.

Spontaneous by nature, Clinton aspires to travel to many amazing destinations around the world with an adventure across South America to visit friends and indulge his love of football next on the list!
